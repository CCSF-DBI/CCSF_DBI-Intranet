Administrative Services

  Purpose

Administrative Services (AS) provides support to the Department in the areas of fiscal management, purchasing, and business analysis. Processes request for refunds for fees collected by Department of Building Inspection. Responsible for keeping employees and the public fully apprised of the Department’s mission and its professional services. AS provides Department-wide automated data capture, data management, and report dissemination. In addition, AS manages, processes and updates all record requests services, prepares Reports of Residential Building Records (3R). AS is responsible for processing 311 customer service requests, updates DBI’s website, and for generating monthly, quarterly and annual reports detailing the Department’s overall performance. Provides support to the Department in the areas of personnel, payroll, and other related Human Resources activities. Administrative Services is also responsible for the Community Outreach Program and the Development Fee Collection Unit.
